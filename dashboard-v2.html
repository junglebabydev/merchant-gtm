Outreach mechanics

Cold emails (Resend): Sent via Resend from mail.jungle.baby. Webhook events flow into email_events in real time.

Vertical identification: The offering_type column in merchant_outreach determines which vertical each merchant belongs to (camps, birthdays, classes). Email events are matched to verticals by subject line.

Sequence stages: Each row in merchant_outreach carries a sequence_stage (which email was last sent) and an outreach_stage (best engagement reached: not_contacted, delivered, opened, clicked).

Response status: no_response, warm, customer. Wins = warm + customer.

Internal filter: Events from vaibhav@jungle.baby and jasmit@jungle.baby are always excluded.
